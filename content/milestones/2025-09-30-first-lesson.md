Today was Maya's very first piano lesson! She sat down at the keys for the first time with her teacher, Mrs. İnci Temir. She learned how to sit properly, how to position her hands, the names of the keys, and the timing of basic notes — C, D, and E on the right hand, and A, B, and C on the left hand. Her first piano book is Kolay Piyano 1 by Sevinç Ereren.
